Micro View Link Sdn Bhd

Company Overview

Founded in 2020, Micro View Link is a dynamic company located in the Penang Free Trade Zone, Malaysia. With an issued and paid-up capital of RM 2.5 million, we specialize in the semiconductor industry, leveraging a team with diverse capabilities and over three years of manufacturing and operations experience.

Manufacturing Capabilities

  • Manufacturing Lines: Three manufacturing lines with a monthly capacity of 100,000 optical modules.
  • Certifications: Our facility is ISO certified.
  • Multi-Language Support: We provide multi-language communication capabilities to cater to our global clientele.

Strategic Advantages

  • Location Benefits: Located just 14 km from the sea port and 6 km from the airport.
  • Supply Chain Efficiency: Established partnerships with local PCBA suppliers for efficient drop shipment services worldwide.
